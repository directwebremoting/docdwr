Commercial Support

SitePen can provide a number of support packages to help you with Ajax/DWR development.

The three primary benefits of our support packages include:

  • Unlimited advice for your designated team members for questions about best practices, approaches, etc.
  • A set number of hours dedicated to fixing problems you have either with DWR itself, or your use of DWR.
  • priority support to get a solution to your problem right away.

For information about commercial support, please contact us. If you have questions about DWR please don't send them to the authors directly. There are far more people that can help on the mailing lists, so you'll get a faster response that way.

Mailing lists

Most of these mailing lists are hosted by java.net. You can join Java.net by registering here, and then you can subscribe. However, you should not need to subscribe to take part - web interfaces are available.

DWR-Users Mailing List

The users mailing list is where the vast majority of discussion about DWR takes place.

**** Before sending a message to the list please take a look at our Getting Started and Troubleshooting documentation and try search the list for an answer. ****

You can send email to the list directly using: [users at dwr.dev.java.net], or you can access the list on the web:

  • Java.net archive of DWR-Users
  • Nabble archive of DWR-Users
  • GMane archive of DWR-Users

DWR-Development

This list is used for DWR developer discussions. This list should not be used for questions (please use the users list for these types of inquiries!). The email address for the list is: [dev at dwr.dev.java.net], or, on the web:
  • Java.net archive of DWR-Dev
  • Nabble archive of DWR-Dev
